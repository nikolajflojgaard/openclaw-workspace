# Humanizer pattern reference

Source basis: categories from Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing" (stylistic tells, over-structured prose, hedging, formulaic transitions).

## Stock phrases to strip or rewrite
- at the end of the day
- it's worth noting
- in today's fast-paced world
- delving into
- tapestry
- robust
- seamless(ly)
- leverage (as vague verb)
- unlock
- game-changer

## Performed authenticity patterns
- let's be real
- honestly
- to be honest
- as an AI
- speaking candidly

## Structural patterns
- em dash overuse
- repeated triads ("X, Y, and Z" chains in consecutive lines)
- padded transitions ("moreover", "furthermore", "additionally")

## Editing principle
Keep the claim, remove the costume.
